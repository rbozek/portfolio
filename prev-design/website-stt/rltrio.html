about.

The Ryan Liatsis Trio is a guitar-led jazz trio from Nyack, NY that applies unique musicality to create the right vibe for any special occasion. The trio plays jazz favorites and instrumental versions of funk, pop and rock tunes that feature the stellar guitar work of Ryan Liatsis. He is supported by Dave Reiss on bass and Rich Bozek on drums. The trio also composes and performs their own more adventurous music under the name Saber Tooth Trio - Check out their original music at www.SaberToothTrio.com.
