"Primitive Magic" was released January 2012. Ferocious and lively 8-song journey through intense peaks and serene valleys, seamlessly interweaving jazz, funk, rock, latin, hip-hop, and avant-garde.

Featuring:
Erik Lawrence on saxophone
Phil Selesnick on keyboards
Paul Hollands on percussion

Recorded at Tedesco Studio in Paramus, NJ
Mixed/mastered at John Guth Productions in Valley Cottage, NY
Original artwork by Beitiak.
